# ProcessPI Streams Overview

The **ProcessPI Streams Module** provides a structured representation of material and energy movement throughout a process plant.

## Capabilities

### Material Streams
- Represent process fluids, gases, vapors, and mixtures.
- Store thermodynamic state variables.
- Track volumetric, mass, and molar flow rates.
- Handle compositions and molecular weights.

### Energy Streams
- Represent heat and work transfer.
- Track equipment duties.
- Support energy balance calculations.

## Available Stream Types

### MaterialStream
Represents physical process streams containing fluids or mixtures.

### EnergyStream
Represents heat and work transfer between process units.
